Clark Street Play Area & Nature Reserve

Get Involved

Welcome to a community maintained green space for neighbours to relax, gather, walk, picnic, play, pray, grow food, and support biodiversity.

Over the past five years, neighbours have planted native trees, berry bushes, vegetables, and pollinator-friendly flowers — creating a growing urban nature reserve. Join us on the second Sunday of every month to do some communal planting and gardening from 12-3PM. Tea flasks and biscuits welcome!

Community Planting & Park in Bloom

Over time, neighbours have planted trees, flowers, fruit bushes and places to sit, turning this corner of St Jude's into a shared play area and nature reserve.

Growing Biodiversity

  • 30+ nesting sparrows (Red List species)
  • Blue tits, great tits, wrens, robins, jays
  • Regular visits from a sparrowhawk
  • Rare grasshopper species
  • Elephant hawk moth caterpillars
